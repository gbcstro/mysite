Information

The "Cargo Loading Optimizer" is a web application designed to determine the optimal set of boxes that should be loaded in a vehicle. This web app is recommended for customers who wish to get the most out of their vehicle when loading boxes. Small e-commerce or courier services may not have a large fleet of vehicles to employ for delivery; this web application may assist them in organizing their cargo deliveries and maximizing profits.

About Us

The developers of this web application were Computer Science students from Holy Angel University. This web application was a contingent part of their senior thesis.
